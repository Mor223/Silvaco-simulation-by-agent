## Common ATLAS Patterns

Confirmed categories include `go atlas`, `mesh infile=...`, direct `mesh/x.mesh/y.mesh`, `region`, `material`, `electrode`, `contact`, `doping`, `models`, `method`, `solve init`, voltage sweep `solve`, `log outf=...`, `log off`, `save outf=...`, `extract`, and `quit`.

For full flows, `02_atlas_simulation.in` must read the `.str` generated by DevEdit or ATHENA. ATLAS direct is a control baseline only.

## Common Luminous / Optical Patterns

The index contains many `luminous`, `beam`, `wavelength`, `optical`, `photocurrent`, and `responsivity` references. Optical decks require explicit wavelength, source, incidence, power, bias, material parameters, and output target confirmation.

## High-Risk Rules

- Do not use `-ascii` or `dbascii`.
- Do not generate `tonyplot` calls.
- Do not assume DevEdit electrode inheritance is correct; warn users to check inherited electrode names in DeckBuild.
- Do not generate final decks with unconfirmed parameters.
- Do not copy full examples; use indexed syntax patterns and reference paths.
